Recipe 1: Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

- 250g spaghetti
- 300g ground beef
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 400g canned tomatoes
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. In a large pot, cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
2. In a separate pan, brown the ground beef with chopped onion and garlic.
3. Add canned tomatoes, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.
4. Serve the sauce over cooked spaghetti.

Recipe 2: Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

- 1 cup butter, softened
- 1 cup white sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions:

1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until smooth.
3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
4. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add this to the butter mixture.
5. Fold in the chocolate chips.
6. Drop rounded tablespoons of cookie dough onto ungreased baking sheets.
7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
